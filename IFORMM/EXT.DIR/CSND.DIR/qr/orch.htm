Orchestra Syntax: Variable Data Types

iname (init variable - initialization only)
kname (control signal - performance time, control rate)
aname (audio signal - performance time, audio rate)
giname (global init variable - initialization only)
gkname (global control signal - performance time, control rate)
ganame (global audio signal - performance time, audio rate)
wname (spectral data - performance time, control rate)

Orchestra Syntax: Instrument Block Statements

instr NN
endin

i/k/ar init iarg
ir tival
